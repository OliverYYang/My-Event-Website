Saving Sydney

In 'Saving Sydney', the lovable animals of Taronga Zoo face their greatest challenge yet. When a greedy developer threatens to turn their beloved sanctuary into a car park, a group of unlikely animal friends band together to thwart the plan. Led by Tilly the Kangaroo and Ollie the Cockatoo, the motley crew embarks on a daring mission to retrieve evidence that will save their home. Through teamwork, courage, and a splash of ingenuity, they prove that even the smallest paws can make a big difference. A heartwarming tale of unity, activism, and the true meaning of home.
